Professional version

I manage programs, activities, and collection development for kids and teens at a busy urban library. I work with families, educators, and folks from other youth-oriented organizations to provide culturally-responsive and developmentally-appropriate services.

Personal version

One of the greatest pleasures of my life is language. I like learning about etymology, the politics of translation, sociolinguistics, you name it. Perhaps relatedly, I also get a thrill from traveling. These days, a lot of my traveling takes place through reading. Both do wonders for the imagination.
